About the Chairman

Leading a Diversified Cambodian Conglomerate

Dr. Tan Monivann is the Founder and Chairman of DeGrand Group, a diversified Cambodian conglomerate headquartered in Phnom Penh. Under his leadership, the group has grown into a portfolio of brands spanning real estate & property valuation, retail & e-commerce, food distribution, automotive care, fashion, wellness, cosmetics, and engineering services.

Guided by the group motto "Stronger Together," Dr. Monivann's approach centers on building a tightly connected ecosystem of local businesses that support one another and create lasting value for the Cambodian market.
